GroundForge

Choose patterns and stitches to generate diagrams for variations of bobbin lace grounds.

Get started

An easy way:
Select a choose stitches link from the gallery with 376 patterns, select stitches at step one and show it. For larger diagrams increase rows and columns in the parameters form.
More easy patterns:
Use a link from the stitches section below, show step one and two. Try other stitches at both steps. To mix stitches choose a larger base pattern.
Advanced options:
The user guide explains two methods to vary on traditional patterns.
Warning:
With complex stitches and large numbers of rows and columns, your browser can get overwhelmed with step two or three.
