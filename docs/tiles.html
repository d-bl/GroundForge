Bias variations: h' / v'; h, v; another direction: h, v. Diagonal fish: h; lotus: h.

Legend:

  • N = natural repeat (with overlapping tiles)
  • h/v = tile with a single row/column (or two)
  • " = custom head/foot-side
  • ...↗ = refers to templates with variants that are the same when the holes are made round by the animation
  • subscript = same matrix, different tiling hence another pattern
