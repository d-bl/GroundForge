Legend:

  • N = natural repeat (with overlapping tiles)
  • " = custom head/foot-side
  • ...↗ = refers to a sheet with variants that are the same when the holes are made as round as possible by the animation.
  • v/* = v works with (0,2) * requires something else for: to apply the chosen stitches in the diagrams. Note that the patch height should match a checker matrix. Neither of the two markers: thread diagram doesnt work at all.
