A thumbnail index for matching sets of diagrams.

The first thumbnails link to several patches that vary in shape or are mirrored versions. Even a basic Torchon ground may represent interesting variations: by pins at unorthodox positions, by unusual angles on the foot sides (check the links that appear along the patches) and by stitch variations causing threads taking different routes than pairs. These patterns are just a tip of the iceberg discovered by TesseLace.com
